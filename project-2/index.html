How To Make Banana Bread

"Easy to make, no need for a mixer."

By: Hayden Costello

Project Summary:

This has been the most popular banana bread recipe on Simply Recipes for over 10 years!, Why? All you need is a mixing bowl, a fork for whisking eggs and a spoon to mix the batter.

Ingredients:

2 to 3 very ripe bananas, peeled, 1/3 cup melted butter, 1 teaspoon baking soda, Pinch of salt, 3/4 cup of sugar (1/2 cup if you like it less sweet, 1 cup if more sweet), 1 large egg, beaten, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1 1/2 cups of all purpose flour,

Instructions:

Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350 deg. F (150 deg. C), and butter a 4x8-inch pan.
2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until completely smooth. Stir in the melted butter into the mashed bananas.
3. Mix in the baking soda and salt. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mis in the flour.
4. Pour in the batter into your prepared loaf pan. Bake for 50 minutes to 1 hour at 350 deg. F (175 deg. C), or until a tester inserted into the center comes out clean.
5. Remove from oven and let cool in pan for a few minutes. Then remove the banana bread from the pan and let cool completely before serving. Slice and serve.
