How To Make Banana Bread

By: Hayden Costello

Project Summary:

This has been the most popular banana bread recipe on Simply Recipes for over 10 years!, Why? All you need is a mixing bowl, a fork for whisking eggs and a spoon to mix the batter.

Ingredients:

  • 2 to 3 very ripe bananas, peeled
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup of sugar (1/2 cup if you like it less sweet, 1 cup if more sweet)
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
